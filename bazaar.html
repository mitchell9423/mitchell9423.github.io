The Bazaar - Tempo Games

Project Overview:

As a Platform Engineer and Gameplay Programmer at Tempo Games, I played a pivotal role in the development of The Bazaar, an innovative online multiplayer card game. My responsibilities spanned platform engineering and gameplay programming, ensuring both the backend systems and game mechanics provided a seamless and immersive player experience.

Role and Contributions:

  • Platform Engineering: I handled key platform responsibilities, including maintaining build pipelines, setting up automated testing environments, and ensuring cross-platform compatibility across devices. I developed critical backend components to handle high traffic volumes and scalability, ensuring that the game could operate smoothly even under heavy user loads.
  • Gameplay Programming: I worked on designing and implementing core gameplay mechanics, working closely with designers to translate ideas into code. This involved creating dynamic combat systems, balancing mechanics, and integrating new content updates into the gameplay loop. My role also involved performance optimization, ensuring smooth gameplay across multiple platforms.

Technologies Used:

  • Languages: C++, Python
  • Platforms: AWS, Jenkins, Azure DevOps
  • Tools: Unity3D, Version Control (Git), Agile Methodologies

Key Features Developed:

  • Cross-platform multiplayer functionality
  • Combat and card mechanics systems
  • Real-time networked gameplay with optimized server-client architecture
  • Performance optimizations for smooth gameplay across devices
