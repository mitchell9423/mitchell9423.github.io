SkyGods - Aerial Racing and Combat

SkyGods is an adrenaline-fueled aerial racing and combat game set in a post-WWII world. Players pilot highly customizable planes, engaging in high-stakes races and dogfights. With a deep progression system and multiplayer mode, players ascend ranks to achieve the title of SkyGod.

My Role in SkyGods

As the **Lead Developer** and **Project Manager** of SkyGods, I was responsible for overseeing all aspects of the game’s development from concept to completion. Below are some key responsibilities and contributions I made to the project:

  • Game Design & Conceptualization: Designed the core game mechanics, including aerial racing, combat systems, and plane customization. Created the rank-based progression system that encourages player advancement through multiplayer tournaments and races.
  • Programming & Development: Developed the game using Unreal Engine, implementing multi-threaded systems for dynamic plane physics, AI behavior, and real-time multiplayer functionality. Leveraged C++ and Blueprint scripting to create an optimized, high-performance experience.
  • Multiplayer Integration: Integrated robust multiplayer features, enabling both competitive and cooperative modes. Designed custom matchmaking systems and ensured seamless synchronization across all player platforms.
  • Plane Customization & Upgrades: Built a system allowing players to modify and upgrade their planes with parts that can be transferred across multiple planes. Designed unique upgrade paths based on player rank and progression.
  • AI Systems: Implemented advanced AI pilots that mimic real player behavior during races and combat, providing a challenging experience in both single-player and multiplayer modes.
  • Project Management: Managed a small team of artists, sound designers, and other developers to ensure the project stayed on schedule and within scope. Conducted regular sprint meetings and coordinated testing phases.
  • Testing & Optimization: Ensured the game was optimized for performance across different platforms. Worked with testers to identify and fix bugs, improve user experience, and balance the game’s difficulty.

Key Features

  • High-speed: Aerial races in dynamic environments.
  • Combat: Mechanics with strategic use of terrain and weapon pickups.
  • Progression: System from Rookie to SkyGod with unique plane upgrades.
  • Multiplayer: Modes including time trials, last-man-standing, and battle royale.
  • Syndicate System: Underground races, allowing faction-based competition.
